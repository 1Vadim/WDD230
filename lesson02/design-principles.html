White Space and Clean Design

Woven Magazine

wovenmagazine.com

It is used proper white space, padding, and used the proper space between elements of a design. Readability of the page is on high level. It has few clear options to choose. Appropriate spaces between words, text and options names.

Hick's Law & Fitt's Law

Domino's Pizza

dominos.ua

There are few clear direct option for user to choose in order to make an order. So to make decision what to buy takes minimum time and efforts. Every button and other necessary information is on the same viewport, no need to scroll the screen.

Proximity

The Walt Disney Company

thewaltdisneycompany.com

There is a good balance between content, font size, colors, spaces between items on a page.
